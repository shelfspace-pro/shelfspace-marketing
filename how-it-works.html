How It Works

The inventory and payments
transformation

From contracts to weekly checks — here's exactly how ShelfSpace turns consignment into a seamless, automated system.

01
Standardized Framework

Contracts

ShelfSpace streamlines the legal relationship between Vendor and Retailer. Our purpose-built agreement automates the hard stuff — payments, discounts, and shrinkage policies — while ensuring compliance with regulatory and tax agencies.

We turn complex liabilities into a secure, standardized partnership so you can focus on moving product.

02
Zero-Friction Fulfillment

Inventory Transfers

Inventory flows through your supply chain just like standard wholesale. The product is delivered, manifested in METRC, and received into your POS using your current protocols.

You simply load the cost, set the target price, and publish to your menu. We integrate into the background of your operation, ensuring that the only thing that changes is the efficiency of your capital.

03
Unified Customer Experience

Product Sales

Customers purchase items just like any other product. They browse the menu, make their selection, and complete the transaction with zero friction.

These SKUs are indistinguishable from wholesale inventory, ensuring a consistent workflow for your staff and a standard experience for your clientele.

04
Transparent Data & Insights

Weekly Reporting

ShelfSpace ties into your POS to ingest sales and inventory data once a week. Our Engine handles the math — aggregating vendor sales, applying the profit split, and reconciling on-hand inventory.

The result is a unified report giving you a complete financial snapshot, available for download in your portal whenever you need it.

Five-page settlement report: Vendor payout table, returns detail, discount audit with budget vs. actual reconciliation, remaining inventory with JIT velocity metrics, and a depositable check.
05
Automated Weekly Settlements

Weekly Payments

ShelfSpace acts as your central payments coordinator to ensure consistent cash flow. Every week, the system generates a check for each Vendor based on the prior week's confirmed sales.

These checks are instantly available for download in the Vendor Portal. Fully compliant with the Federal Check 21 Act, our checks are legally valid to be printed on standard white paper with black ink on any at-home printer, and can be deposited immediately via mobile banking apps or in-person with a teller.

Check 21 Compliant: Every check is a valid "Substitute Check" under Federal Law. UCC Article 3 Compliant. Print-at-Home Legal. Mobile Deposit Ready.
06
Neutral Mediation

Disputes

ShelfSpace serves as the neutral intermediary — the "Switzerland" of your supply chain. Our role is to act as an objective third party, strictly adhering to the Master Agreement and the empirical data.

Should questions or conflicts arise, our goal is rapid resolution and restored alignment. We review the transaction logs, facilitate communication, and use data-driven insights to resolve the issue fairly, bringing all parties back into harmony.
